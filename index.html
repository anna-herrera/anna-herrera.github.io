About Me

Hi I'm Anna! I am a senior at the Georgia Institute of Technology pursuing a B.S. degree in Computer Science with a concentration in Systems Architecture and Information Internetworks.

Outside of school, I love exploring whatever city I am living in, this summer being Pleasanton, CA and neighboring San Francisco, but during the school year I am based in Atlanta, GA.
